Multi-Objective Weights

Adjust how the scheduler prioritises competing objectives. Values are relative weights — the system normalises them internally.

Weight Guide

W1 Makespan — Prioritise jobs that complete earliest overall. Good for throughput-focused factories.
W2 Delay — Prioritise the most overdue jobs first. Good for on-time delivery KPIs.
W3 Profit — Prioritise highest-margin jobs. Good for revenue-focused operations.
W4 Utilization — Balance load across machines and workers. Good for avoiding bottlenecks.
